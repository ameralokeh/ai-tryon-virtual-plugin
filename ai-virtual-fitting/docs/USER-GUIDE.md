## Introduction

### What is AI Virtual Fitting?

AI Virtual Fitting is an innovative technology that allows you to try on wedding dresses virtually using artificial intelligence. Instead of physically trying on multiple dresses, you can upload your photo and see how different wedding dress styles look on you‚Äîall from the comfort of your home.

The technology uses Google AI Studio's advanced Gemini 2.5 Flash Image model to create realistic virtual try-on experiences. The AI analyzes your photo and seamlessly blends the selected wedding dress onto your image, giving you a realistic preview of how the dress would look on you.

### How It Works

The virtual fitting process is powered by sophisticated AI technology that:

1. **Analyzes Your Photo**: The AI examines your uploaded photo to understand your body shape, pose, and positioning
2. **Processes the Dress**: The system retrieves high-quality images of the selected wedding dress from multiple angles
3. **Creates the Fitting**: Advanced AI algorithms blend the dress onto your photo, adjusting for lighting, shadows, and perspective
4. **Generates the Result**: Within 30-60 seconds, you receive a realistic image showing how the dress looks on you

The entire process is automated and requires no technical knowledge‚Äîsimply upload your photo, select a dress, and let the AI do the work!

## Using Virtual Fitting

### Step-by-Step Process

Follow these simple steps to create your virtual fitting:

#### Step 1: Select a Wedding Dress

**Browse Available Dresses**
1. Look at the product slider on the virtual fitting page
2. Use the left/right arrows to browse through available dresses
3. Click on a dress thumbnail to see it larger
4. Read the dress description and details

**Choosing the Right Dress**
- Consider the style you're interested in (A-line, ball gown, mermaid, etc.)
- Look at the dress details (neckline, sleeves, length)
- Note any special features (lace, beading, train)
- Start with styles you're most curious about


#### Step 2: Upload Your Photo

**Preparing Your Photo**
- Use a recent, clear photo of yourself
- Ensure good lighting and a plain background
- Stand straight, facing the camera
- Full body or upper body shot (head to below waist minimum)

**Uploading Process**
1. Click the **"Upload Photo"** or **"Choose File"** button
2. Browse your device for the photo
3. Select the image file
4. Wait for the upload to complete (usually a few seconds)
5. Preview your uploaded photo

**Supported Formats**
- JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg)
- PNG (.png)
- WebP (.webp)

**File Size Limits**
- Maximum file size: 10MB
- Recommended size: 1-5MB for faster upload
- Minimum resolution: 800x600 pixels
- Recommended resolution: 1920x1080 pixels or higher

**Upload Tips**
- Use WiFi for faster uploads if available
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Don't close the browser during upload
- Wait for confirmation before proceeding

#### Step 3: Review Your Selection

Before processing, verify:
- **Selected Dress**: Confirm you've chosen the dress you want to try
- **Uploaded Photo**: Check that your photo uploaded correctly
- **Credit Balance**: Ensure you have at least 1 credit available
- **Photo Quality**: Make sure the photo is clear and well-lit

**Making Changes**
- To change the dress: Click on a different dress in the slider
- To change your photo: Click "Upload Different Photo"
- To cancel: Click "Cancel" or navigate away

#### Step 4: Start Virtual Fitting

**Processing Your Request**
1. Click the **"Try On This Dress"** button
2. Confirm you want to use 1 credit
3. The system will:
   - Deduct 1 credit from your balance
   - Send your photo and dress selection to the AI
   - Begin processing (typically 30-60 seconds)

**During Processing**
- A progress indicator will show the status
- Don't close the browser window
- Don't navigate away from the page
- Be patient‚ÄîAI processing takes time for quality results

**Processing Time**
- Typical: 30-60 seconds
- May vary based on:
  - Server load
  - Image complexity
  - Internet connection speed
  - AI processing queue


#### Step 5: View Your Results

**Result Display**

Once processing is complete, you'll see:
- Your virtual fitting result image
- The dress blended onto your photo
- Realistic lighting and shadows
- Natural-looking composition

**Viewing Options**
- **Full Screen**: Click to view in full screen mode
- **Zoom**: Use zoom controls to see details
- **Pan**: Click and drag to move around the image
- **Compare**: View side-by-side with original photo (if available)

**Evaluating the Result**

**What to Look For**

## Photo Guidelines

Taking the right photo is crucial for the best virtual fitting results. Follow these guidelines to ensure optimal quality.

### Lighting Requirements

**Natural Light is Best**
- Take photos during daytime near a window
- Use soft, diffused natural light
- Avoid direct harsh sunlight
- Overcast days provide excellent even lighting

**Indoor Lighting**
- Use multiple light sources to avoid shadows
- Position lights in front of you, not behind
- Avoid single overhead lights (create harsh shadows)
- Use soft white bulbs, not yellow or colored lights

**What to Avoid**
- ‚ùå Backlighting (light behind you)
- ‚ùå Harsh shadows on face or body
- ‚ùå Very dark or very bright areas
- ‚ùå Mixed color temperatures (warm and cool lights)
- ‚ùå Flash photography (creates harsh shadows)

**Lighting Checklist**
- ‚úÖ Even lighting across your face and body
- ‚úÖ No harsh shadows
- ‚úÖ Natural skin tones visible
- ‚úÖ Clear details in clothing
- ‚úÖ Consistent brightness throughout

### Background Recommendations

**Ideal Backgrounds**
- Plain, solid-colored walls
- Neutral colors: white, gray, beige, light blue
- Clean, uncluttered space
- Smooth surfaces (not textured)

**Background Colors**
- **Best**: White, light gray, cream
- **Good**: Soft pastels, light neutrals
- **Avoid**: Dark colors, busy patterns, multiple colors

**What to Avoid**
- ‚ùå Busy or cluttered backgrounds
- ‚ùå Patterns, wallpaper, or decorations
- ‚ùå Other people or pets in frame
- ‚ùå Windows or mirrors in background
- ‚ùå Furniture or objects
- ‚ùå Outdoor backgrounds with varying elements

**Background Tips**
- Stand 3-4 feet away from the wall
- Ensure good contrast between you and background
- Remove any visible objects or decorations
- Use a plain sheet or backdrop if needed


### Pose and Position

**Standing Position**
- Stand straight with good posture
- Face the camera directly
- Keep shoulders level and relaxed
- Arms at your sides or slightly away from body
- Feet shoulder-width apart
- Weight evenly distributed

**Camera Position**
- Camera at chest or shoulder height
- Straight-on angle (not from above or below)
- Centered in the frame
- 6-10 feet away from camera
- Use a tripod or stable surface (not handheld)

**Body Positioning**
- Show full body or at minimum head to below waist
- Keep arms visible and away from body
- Stand naturally, not stiff
- Slight smile or neutral expression
- Look at the camera
- Avoid crossed arms or hands on hips

**What to Avoid**
- ‚ùå Sitting or leaning
- ‚ùå Extreme angles or poses
- ‚ùå Turned to the side
- ‚ùå Arms crossed or hidden
- ‚ùå Slouching or hunching
- ‚ùå Looking away from camera

**Framing**
- Leave some space above your head
- Include full body if possible
- Center yourself in the frame
- Ensure nothing is cut off
- Keep the frame vertical (not tilted)

### Quality Requirements

**Image Resolution**
- **Minimum**: 800x600 pixels
- **Recommended**: 1920x1080 pixels (Full HD)
- **Ideal**: 2560x1440 pixels or higher
- Higher resolution = better results

**Image Clarity**
- Photo must be in focus
- No motion blur
- Sharp details visible
- Clear facial features
- Crisp edges

**File Format**
- **Supported**: JPEG, PNG, WebP
- **Recommended**: JPEG for photos
- **Quality**: High quality, minimal compression
- **Size**: Under 10MB

**Technical Quality**
- No pixelation or artifacts
- Good color accuracy
- Proper exposure (not too dark or bright)
- No digital noise or grain
- Original photo (not heavily edited)


### Clothing in Photo

**What to Wear**
- Form-fitting clothing (not baggy)
- Solid colors preferred
- Simple, plain garments
- Clothing that shows your body shape
- Tank top, fitted t-shirt, or similar

**Colors to Wear**
- Neutral colors work best
- Avoid white (blends with background)
- Avoid very dark colors
- Medium tones are ideal

**What to Avoid**
- ‚ùå Loose, baggy clothing
- ‚ùå Busy patterns or prints
- ‚ùå Bulky sweaters or jackets
- ‚ùå Clothing with large logos
- ‚ùå Accessories that obscure body shape

### Photo Examples

**Good Photo Example:**
- ‚úÖ Natural, even lighting
- ‚úÖ Plain white background
- ‚úÖ Standing straight, facing camera
- ‚úÖ Arms slightly away from body
- ‚úÖ Clear, in-focus image
- ‚úÖ Full body visible
- ‚úÖ Neutral expression
- ‚úÖ Form-fitting clothing

**Poor Photo Example:**
- ‚ùå Harsh shadows or backlighting
- ‚ùå Busy background with objects
- ‚ùå Sitting or unusual pose
- ‚ùå Arms crossed or hidden
- ‚ùå Blurry or out of focus
- ‚ùå Only partial body visible
- ‚ùå Extreme facial expression
- ‚ùå Baggy or patterned clothing

### Quick Photo Checklist

Before uploading, verify:
- [ ] Good, even lighting with no harsh shadows
- [ ] Plain, solid-colored background
- [ ] Standing straight, facing camera
- [ ] Arms visible and away from body
- [ ] Clear, in-focus image
- [ ] High resolution (800x600 minimum)
- [ ] Proper file format (JPEG, PNG, or WebP)
- [ ] File size under 10MB
- [ ] Form-fitting clothing
- [ ] Recent photo (within last few months)

## Troubleshooting

### Upload Issues

**Problem: Photo Won't Upload**

**Possible Causes:**
- File size too large (over 10MB)
- Unsupported file format
- Poor internet connection
- Browser issues

**Solutions:**
1. **Check File Size**
   - Verify file is under 10MB
   - Compress image if needed
   - Use online image compressor tools

2. **Verify File Format**
   - Ensure file is JPEG, PNG, or WebP
   - Convert to JPEG if using other format
   - Check file extension is correct

3. **Check Internet Connection**
   - Test internet speed
   - Switch to WiFi if using mobile data
   - Try again when connection is stable
   - Close other bandwidth-heavy applications

4. **Try Different Browser**
   - Clear browser cache and cookies
   - Try Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
   - Disable browser extensions temporarily
   - Update browser to latest version

**Problem: Upload Starts But Fails**

**Solutions:**
- Don't close browser during upload
- Wait for upload to complete fully
- Check for error messages
- Verify file isn't corrupted
- Try uploading a different photo

**Problem: "Invalid Image" Error**

**Solutions:**
- Ensure image is a real photo (not a drawing or screenshot)
- Check image isn't corrupted
- Verify image has proper headers
- Try re-saving image in a photo editor
- Use a different photo

### Processing Errors

**Problem: Processing Takes Too Long**

**Normal Processing Time:** 30-60 seconds

**If Longer:**
- Wait up to 2 minutes before taking action
- Check internet connection
- Don't close browser window
- Look for progress indicators
- Check for error messages

**If Still Processing After 2 Minutes:**
- Refresh the page
- Check credit balance (may have been deducted)
- Try again with same or different photo
- Contact support if problem persists


**Problem: "Processing Failed" Error**

**Possible Causes:**
- API service temporarily unavailable
- Image quality issues
- Server overload
- Network interruption

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Credit Balance**
   - Verify if credit was deducted
   - If not deducted, try again
   - If deducted, contact support

2. **Try Again**
   - Wait a few minutes
   - Use the same photo
   - Select the same dress
   - Process again

3. **Use Different Photo**
   - Try a different, high-quality photo
   - Ensure it meets all guidelines
   - Process with new photo

4. **Contact Support**
   - If problem persists after 2-3 attempts
   - Provide error message details
   - Include timestamp of attempts

**Problem: "API Error" Message**

**Solutions:**
- This indicates a temporary service issue
- Wait 5-10 minutes and try again
- Check if service status page exists
- Contact support if persistent
- Your credit should not be deducted for API errors

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: What is virtual fitting?**

A: Virtual fitting is an AI-powered technology that allows you to see how wedding dresses look on you without physically trying them on. You upload your photo, select a dress, and the AI creates a realistic image of you wearing that dress.

**Q: How accurate is the virtual fitting?**

A: The virtual fitting provides a good representation of how a dress style will look on you, including the silhouette, neckline, and overall appearance. However, it's a guide, not a perfect replica. We recommend using it to narrow down your choices before in-person fittings.

**Q: Do I need special equipment?**

A: No special equipment is needed. You just need:
- A smartphone or camera to take your photo
- A computer or mobile device with internet access
- A web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)

**Q: Is my photo stored or shared?**

A: Your photos are processed securely and used only for creating your virtual fitting results. Photos are not shared with third parties. Temporary files are automatically deleted after processing. Check the website's privacy policy for complete details.

**Q: Can I use the same photo for multiple dresses?**

A: Yes! Once you upload a photo, you can use it to try on multiple dresses. You don't need to upload it again unless you want to use a different photo.

### Credit Questions

**Q: How many free credits do I get?**

A: New users receive 2 free credits automatically when creating an account. This allows you to try the virtual fitting feature twice before purchasing more credits.

**Q: How much do additional credits cost?**

A: The standard package is 20 credits for $10.00 USD, which works out to $0.50 per virtual fitting. Check the website for current pricing and any special offers.

**Q: Do credits expire?**

A: By default, credits do not expire. However, the store may configure an expiration policy. Check your account or contact support for specific details about credit expiration.

**Q: What happens if processing fails?**

A: If processing fails due to a system error before the AI starts working, your credit typically won't be deducted. If you believe a credit was deducted incorrectly, contact support with the transaction details.

**Q: Can I get a refund on credits?**

A: Credit refunds are subject to the store's refund policy. Contact customer support with your order number and reason for the refund request.


### Photo Questions

**Q: What kind of photo should I use?**

A: Use a clear, recent photo of yourself with:
- Good, even lighting
- Plain, solid-colored background
- Standing straight, facing the camera
- Full body or upper body visible
- High resolution (800x600 minimum)
- Form-fitting clothing

**Q: Can I use a professional photo?**

A: Yes! Professional photos often work very well because they typically have good lighting and composition. Just ensure the photo meets all the guidelines (plain background, standing pose, etc.).

**Q: Can I use a selfie?**

A: Selfies can work, but they're not ideal because:
- Usually taken from an angle
- Often don't show enough of the body
- May have inconsistent lighting
- Typically lower quality

For best results, have someone else take your photo or use a tripod with a timer.

**Q: What if I don't have a full-body photo?**

A: A photo showing at least from your head to below your waist can work. However, full-body photos provide better results because the AI can see your complete proportions and create a more accurate virtual fitting.

**Q: Can I edit my photo before uploading?**

A: Light editing is okay (adjusting brightness, cropping), but avoid:
- Heavy filters or effects
- Significant color changes
- Body modifications
- Adding or removing elements

The more natural your photo, the better the results.
